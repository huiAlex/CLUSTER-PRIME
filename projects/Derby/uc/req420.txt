[DERBY-4608] Unnecessary conversion of binary values to strings in SQLBinary.compare() <p>SQLBinary.compare(int,DataValueDescriptor,boolean,boolean) converts the values to strings in order to check whether any of them are null. The isNull() method should be used instead to prevent the unnecessary conversion to strings.</p>

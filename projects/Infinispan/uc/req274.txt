[ISPN-5120] Expose EmbeddedCacheManager/Cache via JCacheManager/JCache unwrap(...) methods <p>The JSR-107 spec includes the CacheManager.unwrap(Class) and Cache.unwrap(Class) methods to allow access to underlying caching implementation.  Currently, I can only unwrap the Cache/CacheManager instance to the concrete JCache/JCacheManager implementations.  However, these classes do not expose the underlying infinispan Cache/EmbeddedCacheManager.</p>

<p>Ideally, I should be able to do the following:</p>

A solution would be JMX as described here: <a href= http://lists.jboss.org/pipermail/infinispan-dev/2011-March/007651.html  class= external-link  rel= nofollow >http://lists.jboss.org/pipermail/infinispan-dev/2011-March/007651.html</a></p>

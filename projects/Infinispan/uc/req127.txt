[ISPN-1794] Make connection refused exceptions TRACE <p>This is just a proposal, feel free to reject.</p>

<p>Default behaviour in PropsKeyedObjectPoolFactory is<br/>
minIdle = 1<br/>
timeBetweenEvictionRunsMillis = 2min<br/>
i.e. after 2minutes of idle RemoteCacheManager will try to ensure at least one connection and will try to create one.<br/>
This might be a surprise for user to get this WARNING without doing anything:</p>

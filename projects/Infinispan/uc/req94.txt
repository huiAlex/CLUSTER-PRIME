[ISPN-1198] Cache entrySet,keySet and values operations ignoring expired entries <p>Cache s entrySet/keySet/values implementations do not take into account that entries might have expired, so the collections returned might contain key/value pairs that should be removed.</p>

<p>This has the side effect of getBulk remote cache operation returning entries even after their lifespan ends. From Martin:</p>

<blockquote><p>This is related to a getBulk operation on an Infinispan HotRod client.</p>
